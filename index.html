Hong Wang

Ph.D. Student · Bellini College of AI, Cybersecurity & Computing
University of South Florida  ·  RARE Lab

I am a third-year Computer Science Ph.D. student advised by Professor Zhao Han. Before USF, I received my B.S. in Information & Computing Science from Hohai University (2020) and my M.S. in Computer Science from Stony Brook University (2022).

My research lies at the intersection of robotics and augmented reality, investigating how AR and projector-camera systems can enhance the transparency and explainability of robotic systems to support more intuitive human–robot interaction.
